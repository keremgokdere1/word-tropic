

Privacy Policy for Word Tropic

Last updated: July 4, 2026

This Privacy Policy explains how Word Tropic collects, uses, and protects information when you use our mobile game.

1. Information We Collect

Word Tropic does not require users to create a custom account inside the app.

However, the app may use third-party services that collect certain information automatically, including:

- Device information
- Advertising identifiers
- Approximate location derived from IP address
- App usage and interaction data
- Crash logs and performance data
- Ad views and ad interactions

These data types may be collected by third-party services used in the app, such as Google AdMob and Apple Game Center.

2. Advertising

Word Tropic uses Google AdMob to show ads, including rewarded ads and native ads.

Google AdMob may collect and use data to:
- Show ads
- Measure ad performance
- Prevent fraud and abuse
- Improve advertising services
- Provide personalized or non-personalized ads depending on user consent and device settings

You can learn more about how Google uses data here:
https://policies.google.com/technologies/ads

3. Rewarded Ads

Word Tropic may allow users to watch rewarded ads to earn in-game rewards such as coins or lives. Rewards are provided only after a rewarded ad is successfully completed.

4. Game Center

Word Tropic may use Apple Game Center for multiplayer and duel features. If you use Game Center, Apple may process your Game Center profile, nickname, achievements, scores, or multiplayer-related information according to Apple’s privacy practices.

You can learn more about Apple’s privacy practices here:
https://www.apple.com/legal/privacy/

5. In-Game Data

Word Tropic may store gameplay-related data locally on your device, such as:
- Level progress
- Coins
- Lives
- Game settings
- Language preference
- Music preference

This information is used only to provide and improve the game experience.

6. Children’s Privacy

Word Tropic is designed as a general audience word puzzle game. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children. If you believe that a child has provided personal information, please contact us so we can take appropriate action.

7. Data Sharing

We do not sell personal information. Data may be processed by third-party services such as Google AdMob and Apple Game Center as described above.

8. User Choices

You can limit ad tracking or reset your advertising identifier through your device settings.

You can also manage Game Center settings through your Apple device settings.

9. Security

We take reasonable measures to protect information related to the app. However, no method of electronic storage or transmission is completely secure.

10. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted on this page with an updated “Last updated” date.
